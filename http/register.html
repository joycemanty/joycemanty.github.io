Privacy Notice
In becoming a patient of the UTS Health Service, we collect your personal and health information to provide for optimal ongoing management of your health, care and wellbeing and to ensure this Practice is able to continue treating you as a patient. If you choose not to disclose information that may be necessary to providing this service to you, the type or quality of care provided will be affected.

Health information will be collected directly from you, or with your consent, from other parties including GPs, specialists, hospitals, dentists, pathology, radiology and allied health providers who may be involved in your care.

All patients have the right to request access to their own personal health information. If you wish to access a copy of your records, please contact the Practice on (02) 9514 1177. The Practice provides limited information about you to Medicare for the purpose of accessing and processing Medicare financial rebates where applicable.

As a patient of this Practice we may need to disclose, with your consent, relevant personal health information in order to provide you with access to the best ongoing health care available both in acute and chronic situations, such as with GPs, specialists, hospitals, dentists, pathology, radiology and allied health providers who may be involved in your care.

This Practice may also be legally required or permitted in certain circumstances to disclose patient information under legislative obligations, including the Infectious Diseases Act - Health (Infectious Diseases) Regulations, Adoption Act, where a court order, subpoena, or legal warrant is received, for quality assurance or accreditation purposes.

Please contact the Practice Manager if you have any questions or a complaint regarding privacy and the management of your health information. Further information about privacy generally can be found on the Privacy at UTS website

By signing this form you agree to this Practice and its staff passing on your personal health information on your behalf in order to access ongoing medical care in keeping with privacy legislation and the notice above.
