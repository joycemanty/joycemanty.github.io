Staff Information


We have a team of experienced and approachable doctors. All our doctors are vocationally registered with the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners.

The Health Service is accredited through GPA Accreditation Plus.


Dr Beena Chaugule MB BS

Beena's clinical interests include family medicine and women's health. She has a holistic approach to healing and well-being. Beena is passionate about emphasising a healthy lifestyle and believes that we can all make a impact on our bodies through the ways in which we live. She has worked for many years as a GP and has experience in a emergency medicine.
Outside of medicine she is in the ocean daily and is a regular yoga practitioner.


Dr Albert Ling MB BS, FRACGP

Albert has been appointed as an examiner for the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners. He enjoys music, the outdoors, travel and an occasional beer.


Dr Leonie Matthews MB BS, FRACGP

Dr Leonie Matthews graduated from UNSW and has more than twenty years' experience in general practice. She is a Fellow of RACGP, has a Diploma in Child Health and a Family Planning Association Certificate. Leonie is experienced in all areas of general practice and has a particular interest in women's health, mental health issues and paediatrics. She loves the variety and challenges which general practice offers.


Dr Keping Xu MBBS

Keping has interest in family health medicine, with special interest in mental health and youth health issues. Keping is experienced in skin cancer checks and is is qualified in aviation medicine. In her spare time, Keping enjoys music, reading and travel.
